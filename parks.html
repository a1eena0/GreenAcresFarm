The Playpark:




Bring the entire family round for fun at our vast play park for all ages!

We have a variety of playground equipment with dedicated areas for under 5s, general play and also Go-Karting!

Go-Karting is £7 per person for 4 laps. You must be at least 12 years old to ride by yourself. Please bring change where possible!
